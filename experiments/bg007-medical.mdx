## The Question

Patient 7291, female, age 47. CA-125 at 285 U/mL (8.1x elevation). Complex cystic mass on ultrasound. History of endometriosis. Does she have ovarian cancer or endometriosis?

## The Deliberation

24 agents — oncologists, pathologists, radiologists, surgeons, patient advocates, clinical guidelines databases — debated across 12 rounds using only AXL packets.

Key moments:
- **Round 3:** Oncologist infers malignancy (INF.82) from CA-125 + imaging
- **Round 5:** Pathologist contradicts (CON.65) citing endometriosis history
- **Round 7:** Oncologist yields (YLD.55) — changes from malignant to uncertain
- **Round 10:** Merge (MRG.70) — both viable, biopsy indicated
- **Round 12:** Prediction convergence at 60% malignancy probability
